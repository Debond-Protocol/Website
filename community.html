Debond is above all a community whose aim is to bring real change to DeFi. We have developed the ERC–3475 standard to achive this — making investments safer and more stable.

Unlike other DeFi projects like SnapShot, we do not only poll our community. Debond makes transparent, community-led, decentralised governance possible on our comprehensive platform. Smart contracts and DBGTs (Debond Governance Tokens) are used to vote democratically on proposals, revisions, updates, withdrawals, and executions about Debond and its funds on the chain.
Participate in Debond’s development, let’s create the next-level-DeFi standard together!
